## Why Documentary-Style?

Documentary filmmaking is a special approach to storytelling that supports authentic moments and real human emotions. This unlocks the ability to [connect with your audience on a deeper level](https://www.coalbanks.ca) for things like recruitment, investor relations, or customer engagement.

If you have stories worth telling in your business, this approach will hopefully make creating genuine connections a breeze.
